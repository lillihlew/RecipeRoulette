Recipe Roulette is a web application that generates a recipe for you based on ingredients it recognizes in a photo of your fridge.

  • It can create new and exciting recipes you've never seen before
  • It can help add variety to your diet
  • It can make your life easier!
  • It can reduce food waste by helping you use unwanted ingredients
  • Cooking one of these recipes will create a memory you'll never forget!
